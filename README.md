# Ice Climber
It is a recreation of the famous videogame Ice Climber, developed in Unity. <br><br>
In this game, the player has to climb the mountain dodging obstacles and defeating the enemies that stand in their way. <br>
The game includes the 32 mountains from the original, and about half of its enemies. <br><br>
As part of the project, we implemented telemetry by sending gameplay data to a MongoDB Atlas cloud database, enabling us to analyze user interactions and performance metrics. <br><br>
The game was developed by my friend Alejandro and me in 3 weeks, as the final project of our first year of the Higher National Diploma in Multiplatform Application Development. <br>
